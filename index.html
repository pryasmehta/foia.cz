About FOIA

FRIENDS OF INDIA z. s. supports the development, enrichment and improvement of relations between India and the Czech Republic in cultural, social, educational and economic areas.

Main Activities

  • Organizing thematic events, meetings of members and guests to exchange experiences and build relationships.
  • Developing relations between the Czech Republic and India and cooperating with cultural and economic organizations.
  • Initiating activities supporting international cooperation and exchange of information between the two countries.
  • Supporting research, education, universities and cultural development aligned with the association’s mission.

Goal of the Association

The goal of the association is cooperation with entrepreneurs, government authorities and institutions in order to strengthen mutually beneficial relations between the Czech Republic and India.

Added Value

The added value of FRIENDS OF INDIA z. s. is the active development of contacts, strengthening international relations and supporting long-term cooperation between Czech and Indian partners.
